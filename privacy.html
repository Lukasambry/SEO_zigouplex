Privacy Policy

At Zigouplex, we are committed to protecting your privacy and securing your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information.

1. Introduction

This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, the protective measures we implement, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data Collection

We collect various categories of data to enhance our services and personalize your experience:

  • Personal Data: Name, first name, email address, phone number, etc., voluntarily provided by you during registration or use of our services.
  • Browsing Data: Information automatically collected during your visit to our site, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on each page, etc.
  • Location Data: Geographical information based on your IP address or your location settings if you have enabled them.
  • Transaction Data: Information related to purchases or transactions made on our site, including payment details.
  • Communication Data: Correspondence between you and our customer service, including emails, chats, or phone calls.

3. Data Usage

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To process your requests, manage your account, and provide our services.
  • Personalization: To tailor our content and offers based on your preferences and browsing history.
  • Communication: To send you notifications, updates, newsletters, and promotional offers, subject to your consent.
  • Site Improvement: To analyze site usage and improve its performance, security, and usability.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our site, conducting our business, or serving you, provided these parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, we may disclose your personal data to comply with a legal process, court order, or government request.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Zigouplex, our users, or the public.

5. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal data. These measures include encryption protocols, firewalls, and strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your information.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request the restriction of processing your personal data in certain cases.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request to receive your personal data in a structured and commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the following address: privacy@zigouplex.tech.

7. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to enhance your user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help remember your preferences and analyze site usage.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect certain functionalities of our site.

For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

8. Security

We take the security of your data very seriously and employ advanced technologies to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

9. Policy Changes

Zigouplex reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will take effect upon their publication on the site. It is your responsibility to regularly review this page to stay informed of any updates.
